From Recruiter to Case Manager – My Journey

I began my career in 2011 as a recruiter, working closely with candidates and understanding the real dynamics of matching talent to opportunities. From there, my path evolved into managing customer service teams, case operations, and eventually becoming a Team Manager at Amazon, handling both people and performance across various geographies.

My ability to solve people and process issues, create feedback loops, and influence onboarding and learning outcomes naturally shaped me into a delivery-focused leader. I discovered Agile methodologies along the way, not as buzzwords — but as practical approaches to fixing what was broken, improving what could be better, and scaling what worked.

My learning didn’t stop at leadership. I ventured into Scrum, earned certifications, and even built this very website myself using HTML, CSS, GitHub, Netlify, and VS Code. Through real-time collaboration, I explored how tech and content come together — this page being a living proof of that hands-on journey.

Today, I’m not just someone who’s led operations. I’m a strategic executor, deeply invested in learning, building, and sharing. Whether it's aligning teams to outcomes or presenting ideas through storytelling — my journey continues, and this portal is one stop along the way.
